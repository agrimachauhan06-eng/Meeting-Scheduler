How to get your Google Calendar iCal URL
  1. Open Google Calendar → click the three dots next to your calendar → Settings
  2. Scroll to "Integrate calendar" section
  3. Copy the Secret address in iCal format (starts with https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/...)
  4. Paste it below and click Add Feed
